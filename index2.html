About Our Website

Welcome to our Web application, where you can find each and every detail about the clubs and socities of our college. There are both technical and non technical socities in our college, which recruit students in 1st and 2nd year. But students are unable to decide in which society they want to go, due to lack of information and knowledge. So, to resolve this problem INFOSHALA is build. It provide proper information about socities and provide a form for registering in their recruitment drives. It is basically an informative as well as registeration website. With the help of our website, students will easily grab the opportunity and missing of any information about recruitment will not happen. Stay tuned with our website so that you won't miss any opportunity.
